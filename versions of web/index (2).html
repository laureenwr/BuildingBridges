About the Project

Building bridges to academic futures

"Building Bridges" is a 36-month interdisciplinary research and development project that aims to empower and mentor girls and FLINTA* of color from the 10th grade onwards. The project is being carried out by the Free University of Berlin, the SPI Foundation, and the University of Duisburg-Essen.

The entire project is scientifically monitored by the Free University of Berlin (Clinical Child and Adolescent Psychology and Psychotherapy). Interviews and questionnaires assess experiences of discrimination, mental health, stressors, resources, and academic participation.

Project Goals

Our main goals are to promote & empower

📋

Developing an MEP Program

Empowering BIPoC girls and FLINTA* through a needs-based, intersectionality-sensitive mentoring program.

🎓

Promoting Academic Careers

Promoting academic careers in the psychosocial field and strengthening the resources of M*oC for the path into science.

⭐

Creating Role Models

Integration of mentors and role models of colour to highlight successful educational biographies and empowerment.

🌐

Digital Platform

Creation of a participatory digital platform for context-sensitive storytelling and sustainable peer-to-peer exchange.

🔍

Research Barriers

Investigation of experiences of discrimination and barriers and conditions for increased participation in academic settings.

💪

Strengthening Resilience

Strengthening resilience and performance potential through the identification and activation of individual talents and resources.

Design Principles

Developed through interviews, workshops, and participant feedback — translated into concrete platform decisions.

🔒 Privacy by default

Stories are saved as private drafts. Nothing is published without your explicit choice.

⏭️ Skip anything

Every sensitive question has a "skip" option. No part of the process is mandatory.

🕐 Publish later

You never have to publish immediately. Take as much time as you need to decide.

🌈 Inclusive language

All platform text uses gender-inclusive, accessible language in German and English.

💙 Emotional safety

Content warnings, pause points, and wellbeing check-ins are built into the story flow.

🔍 Transparency

Clear explanations of how AI is used, what data is stored, and who can access your content.
